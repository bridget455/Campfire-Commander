Campsite Commander is an app from Android that assists with managing your Camping tools and gears by sorting them out by a list.
It works when you open the app,the first thing you see is the splash screen but for only 3 seconds.
It shows the Logo and the Title, then it takes you back to the main screen.
On that screen you can enter how many item you will need or how many items you packed.
There are two buttons, one for adding the tools and gears another to view the list.
When you tap Add Gear it takes you to the screen where you can fill in a form you can choose a category (e.g. shelter, food, clothing etc.).
You can also enter the quantity and and to also add notes to it.
When you save the items get stored in parallel Arrays in the background.
When View Gear List is tapped you are taken to the DetailedView where it loops throught all your Arrays and displays every item with its name, category, quantity and notes if added.
Lastly we have a "Back to Base" button which takes us back to the home screen.

In summary Parallel Arrays store data.
Loops calculate the total items and displays the list.
Screen navigatin works for only 3 seconds to diplay the logo and title.
Error handeling checks and validates your input.
finally there is a dark nature theme in this app through out all the screens.
